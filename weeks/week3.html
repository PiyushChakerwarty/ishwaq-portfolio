Information Systems Databases

In today's lesson the teacher has taught us about the concepts of normalisation functional dependency diagrams and denormalization. I have learnt how important normalization is as it helps minimize inconsistency and redundancy in data making sure data integrity and making the data base more efficient. In addition one of the things i learnt is how functional dependency is linked between attributes in relation whereas one attribute uniquely regulates another. One of the things i have also learnt about is denormalization and how it involves deliberately introducing redundancy for presentation by optimization of querying. After the lesson I have drawn a functional dependency diagram to get better understanding of it.
