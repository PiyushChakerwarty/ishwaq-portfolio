Week 4 Reflection


Developing Digital Identity

In today's lesson the lecturer has taught me about the fundamental concepts of CSS and gaining insights into various aspects of styling web pages. Some of the key points I learnt from today's lecture is normalize.css style sheet and how it works by normalizing browser and ensuring stability throughout different browsers. This is executed by providing a steady baseline for styling it makes the development process simpler and improves the user experience by minimizing rendering inconsistencies. I also learnt about the differences between absolute vs. relative units and how important it is understanding how the elements are positioned and sizes in CSS. Absolute unit provides fixed sizes and relative unit provides adaptability and flexibility with different devices and screen sizes.


Information Systems Databases

Today's session was really fascinating as i have learnt about various of aspects on database design and entity relationships. My lecturer has taught us about recursive entity relationships and how the databases are interconnected based on this. Also another important thing i was taught about is 'mutually exclusive relationships' in database and have drawn a diagram to help me understand the purpose of this. I was also able to identify and draw 'subtype' and 'supertype' and understanding how important it is in database system and how it is linked to entities. I was able to identify and solve common connection traps in ER diagram.
